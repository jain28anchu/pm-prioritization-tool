AI Product Strategy Tool

AI-Powered RICE Prioritization Tool

An AI-assisted product prioritization tool that helps teams evaluate initiatives using automated Reach, Impact, Confidence, and Effort scoring.

How It Works

Answer a series of guided product strategy questions around customer impact, business value, confidence, and technical complexity. The tool converts your responses into structured RICE scores to support faster, more consistent prioritization decisions.

Reach

Measures the number of users, customers, or workflows impacted by the initiative.

Impact

Measures the potential business or customer value created if the initiative succeeds.

Confidence

Measures how confident the team is in the available data, assumptions, and expected outcomes.

Effort

Measures the level of engineering, design, operational, and cross-functional work required to execute the initiative.

Prioritize an Initiative

Select the option that best describes the initiative. The tool will calculate a priority recommendation based on Reach, Impact, Confidence, and Effort.
